“Where the world is firm, alteration makes it fluid.
Walls become doors, gravity bends, sight and sound twist.

The Flow is a river; push against it, and it floods the banks.
Bend it gently, and the impossible feels natural.

I have glimpsed a scroll describing a phase veil — a way to step sideways into a version of the world where walls do not exist.
If true, the towers themselves may hide more than mere stone…”
