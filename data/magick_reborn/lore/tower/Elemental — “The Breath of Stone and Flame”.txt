“To speak with the elements is not to command, but to invite.
Fire hungers, water remembers, air wanders, earth waits.
When the Flow threads through them, they answer in kind.

Yet the balance is fragile: too much heat, and the rivers retreat; too much stone, and the winds choke.

The oldest Weavers whispered of the Storm Core — a crystal forged where all four elements clashed and danced as one.
Its location… lost to the tides of time.”
