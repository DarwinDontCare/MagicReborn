[Scrawled in a sharp, hurried hand]

“Life is a flame; death, only the cooling of embers.
Stir the embers, and the fire may flicker again — but it will never burn as before.

The Abyss whispers to those who linger at gravesides. It offers motion without mind, strength without soul.

Some Weavers raised entire hosts from the charnel pits.
None of them smiled again.”
